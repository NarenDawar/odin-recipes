Lasagna Recipe

FInished Product

Lasagna is an amazing dish to make. It has a lot of cheese and can be seen as a more cohesive piece of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Dough in pasta form
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Create the base using dough
  3. Apply some tomato sauce on top of the dough
  4. Sprinkle cheese on top, along with basil
  5. Put in the oven to heat up
