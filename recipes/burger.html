Burger Recipe

Finished Product

Burgers are very famous in the United States. A burger is a combination of many varying elements to make a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Burger (beef)

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Use the bottom bun to stack food onto
  3. Cook the burger accordingly and place it onto the bun.
  4. Apply the cheese next, which will melt from the burgers heat
  5. Place the lettuce on next
  6. PLace the tomatoes and onions last
